Who Can Attend?

REGISTER

This open-to-the-public workshop is intended for:

  • Biotechnology students and researchers from any relevant discipline (BSc, Btech, Msc, MTech, Ph.D.)
  • Genomic scientists.
  • Geneticists and molecular biologists.
  • Life sciences professionals.
  • Bioinformatics professionals.
  • Anyone interested in understanding and mastering the CRISPR Cas9 gene-editing technology can register.

The event will be held at the Prestigious Chott Mariem Institute, WH86+23H, Chott Mariem, Hammam Sousse.
